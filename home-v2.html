Application FAQs

Have any questions? Check our frequently asked questions or reach out to our team at support@acquisition.com.

What kind of businesses do you work with?

At Acquisition, we focus on partnering with growing businesses that have achieved a minimum annual revenue of $3 million and are led by passionate founders. We are looking to collaborate with leaders who are committed to taking their ventures to the next level.

I don’t need money. What else do I get by applying?

Beyond financial support, our collaboration offers a wealth of expertise and resources. By working with us, you gain access to a team of seasoned professionals to provide you strategic guidance, networking opportunities, and hands-on assistance in crucial areas like sales, marketing, hiring, and operations. Our aim is to increase monthly cashflow, build a core leadership team, triple your revenue in 5 years and transform your business into a valuable and sellable enterprise.

How do you achieve those results?

We focus on three key points to unleashing business potential:
WHAT: Figure out the 3 to 5 big initiatives that grow the company’s value the most, and ruthlessly eliminate everything else.
WHO: Recruit talent with a winning track record to breakthrough to new levels of growth.
HOW: Help the new leaders install our step by step proven growth playbooks into each function of the business.

When should I apply?

Applications are welcome at any time. We understand that opportunities for growth can arise unexpectedly, and we are always ready to explore potential partnerships. Whether you're at a pivotal stage in your business or proactively planning for the future, we encourage you to apply whenever you feel ready to embark on a transformative journey.

What are the conditions to receive funding?

The conditions for funding are subject to the unique circumstances of each business. We understand that every venture is distinct, and our evaluation takes into account different factors such as market potential, scalability, goals, and current business performance.

How much do you invest?

Our investment amounts are flexible and tailored to the unique needs of each business. While there is no fixed figure, we are committed to providing the necessary financial support to drive substantial growth. The specific investment details will be discussed during the evaluation and negotiation process to ensure alignment with your business goals.

How can I use the investment money?

The investment received from us is intended to fuel strategic initiatives that drive business growth. Whether it's expanding marketing efforts, optimizing operations, or recruiting talent for your team, we encourage a purposeful allocation of funds. We work collaboratively with business leaders to develop a customized plan that maximizes the impact of the investment and accelerates overall business success.

I’m experiencing technical issues.

If you encounter any technical difficulties during the application process or have issues accessing our platform, please reach out to our dedicated support team at support@acquisition.com.
